Ingredients
  • 200 grams softened butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla flavoring
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups arrowroot flour
  • 1 cup all purpose flourk
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven at 150 degrees Celsius.
  2. In a mixing bowl combine softened butter and granulated sugar with a hand or stand mixer.
  3. Add vanilla flavoring.
  4. Beat in the egg one at a time.
  5. Cream the wet ingredients together until light and fluffy. Then, set it aside.
  6. In a large mixing bowl sift together the arrowroot flour, all-purpose flour and baking powder. Whisk until mixed well.
  7. Stir in the wet ingredients gradually. Mix it in until it has formed a soft dough
  8. Put it on a non stick baking pan or baking sheet then press it with the back of a fork. If you have a cookie press, feel free to use it. Place an inch apart from each other.
  9. In a pre heated oven, baked at 150 degrees Celsius for 10 minutes.
  10. Let it cool on the pan for 5 minutes and then transfer into a wire rack to cool completely. After it has cooled down, you're free to enjoy it with whatever you want!
